Our Service Portfolio

At Velmont Energy, we deliver end-to-end solutions across energy, mining, and mineral sectors to drive sustainable growth and community empowerment.

Energy Solutions

Upstream Operations

Velmont Energy provides comprehensive upstream services that ensure efficient exploration and production:

  • Geological & Seismic Surveys for optimal reserve analysis
  • Advanced drilling and well completion technologies
  • Reservoir management for maximum yield sustainability
  • Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) techniques for mature fields
  • Asset integrity management ensuring operational safety

Downstream Operations

We deliver strategic downstream services to ensure value creation across the supply chain:

  • Refining & processing with state-of-the-art facilities
  • Secure storage, transportation, and distribution networks
  • Export logistics management with global compliance standards
  • Retail and wholesale fuel distribution to power industries

Mining & Minerals

Gold & Diamond Mining

We explore and mine high-grade gold and diamond deposits using ethical and sustainable methodologies to create lasting economic impact in host communities.

Lithium & Rare Earth Minerals

Our lithium and rare earth minerals division is dedicated to supplying critical inputs for Africa’s emerging renewable energy and tech manufacturing industries.

Solid Mineral Trading

We engage in legal, certified buying and global export of precious and industrial stones, complying with international standards to ensure trade transparency.

Sustainability & Community Empowerment

We integrate environmental stewardship and community welfare into every project. Our operations include land rehabilitation, job creation, skills development, and local business empowerment, aligned with Nigeria’s Mining Act and global ESG standards.

Through strategic partnerships, we deploy cutting-edge technologies, including AI and geospatial analytics, to maximise operational efficiency while protecting natural habitats and empowering the communities we serve.
